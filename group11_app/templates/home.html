Track Species Calls and Protect NT Biodiversity

This platform helps researchers and citizen scientists record, review, and monitor species call observations across the Northern Territory. Users can submit recordings, explore recent activity, and flag anomalies for further review.

Submit a Recording View Recent Recordings

What This App Does

Log Recordings

Submit species call recordings with date, location, confidence score, and notes to support biodiversity monitoring.

View Timeline

Browse recent submissions in a timeline-style view to track species activity and identify patterns over time.

Flag Anomalies

Report unusual or suspicious records, such as poor audio quality, incorrect species identification, or duplicate entries.
